MISSION DESCRIPTION

From the beginning of its existence, the Polish space sector has been based mainly on the activities of not only experienced experts, but above all students, willing to cross borders and prove that ideas created in our country have their place above the Kármán Line. This resulted in the creation of not only several full-fledged satellites, but also numerous projects, ventures and research groups, whose members over time became the pillars of this industry in Poland. Young scientists and engineers from AGH, fascinated by the world of technology, wanting to inspire others by implementing innovative missions, therefore join the space race, creating one of the smallest and at the same time the most innovative devices that has ever been launched into orbit by the academic community! The team will not only conduct its own orbital research, but also lay the foundations for the professional development of future Polish specialists in the field of space and satellite engineering at the AGH University of Science and Technology in Krakow!
